Our Story

Where Art, Tradition, and Elegance Unite

R.N White Metals is a trusted silver jewellery brand specializing exclusively in finely crafted anklets. Our designs are rooted in traditional Indian artistry while embracing modern aesthetics.


For more than 20 years, we have proudly served customers across India, delivering anklets that reflect the diverse preferences of different states and regions. By understanding regional taste, we create anklets that feel personal, authentic, and culturally connected.


What began as a small collection has grown into a wide and refined range of anklets, carefully designed for every occasion, including:


  • Daily Wear Silver Anklets
  • Traditional Anklet Designs
  • Bridal & Heavy Anklets
  • Custom Design Anklets

Each anklet is crafted using quality silver and finished with great care to ensure purity, comfort, and long-lasting durability.


At R.N White Metals, we believe jewellery is more than an accessory—it is a symbol of tradition, trust, and timeless elegance.
